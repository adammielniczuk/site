Chess Move Challenge

Can you tell if the chess move was made by a human or a computer?

What is this about?

We are a team of students from Pozna≈Ñ University of Technology working on our engineering thesis. Our goal is to create a chessbot which plays like a human. This test was built to check whether chess players can recognize if a move is made by a human player or our bot, which we named PikeBot. If you wish to learn more feel free to visit the about section or our Github page.

How does it work?

PikeBot has analyzed over half a million of human chess games to learn and understand which moves are played by humans and which ones are not. We use the information it gives us and an evaluation of that postion made by Stockfish to choose what move is most probable.

How to fill the test?

Just choose your chess ELO and press start. You will see two images of a board - one before and one after the move. You need to anwser whether the move was made by a humapn player or not. You don't need to send anything - your anwsers will be automatically submitted. Thank you for your help!
